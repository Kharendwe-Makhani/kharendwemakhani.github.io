This is my CNC dust shoe project.

CNC Dust Shoe

As part of my vacation work at the Department of Mechanical and Mechatronic Engineering at Stellenbosch University I was tasked with designing and creating an accessory that can be used to collect dust and debris from a computer numerical control (CNC) milling machine during the machining process.

I started by designing the accessory in Inventor Autodesk. The accessory included two holes, one for the vacuum pipe and one for the spindle. It also had a channel or tunnel that allowed for dust or debris to travel all the way to the vacuum pipe. The design also include an attachment for bristles that will facilitate in the collection of dust.

After the design was complete, the part was 3D printed, assembled together and tested on the CNC machine.
